In October 2008,   the Gravitational Astrophysics group held "Ozlens 2008"- dark matter, dark energy and dark ages with gravitational lensing. The Conference was well attended with over 80 participants, from both Australian and international institutions.

In addition to five days of highly entertaining talks, we had some lither moments including boomerang throwing and the conference dinner held at the Tea Room QVB.
